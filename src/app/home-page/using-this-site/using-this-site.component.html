Why did I make this site?

Get to know the real Thomas, not the resume Thomas

Rather than a traditional resume written with HTML and CSS type site, I've decided I want the UX of this site to feel like you're having a conversation with me... hopefully I haven't annoyed you yet and that didn't backfire! As you navigate through this site, you should get a good feel for who I am outside of my resume.

Don't understand code? No problem

I plan on hosting several fun little projects on this site, so for those that don't code or don't have the time to clone a repo, you can get a feel for the type of projects I like to build!

Practice, practice, practice!

Whether it's playing the guitar, lifting weights or any other skill I want to learn, I'm a slow and steady type of fella. That's why I like the idea of a never ending project, as it gives me a reason to continually practice my non web-development software skills!
